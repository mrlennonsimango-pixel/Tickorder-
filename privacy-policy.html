TickOrder - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to TickOrder. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • Delivery address for shipping orders
  • Payment information for processing orders
  • Optional information if you subscribe to promotions or newsletters

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders accurately and efficiently
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders, account, or promotions (if you opt-in)
  • To improve our website, products, and services

Information Sharing & Security

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We share your data only with trusted partners for order fulfillment or legal compliance. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

Cookies & Analytics

TickOrder uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, remember preferences, and analyze site traffic. No personally identifiable information is sold or shared.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • You can request corrections or deletions of your personal data.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.
